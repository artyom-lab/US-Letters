TEQUILA COCKTAIL

Drumshanbogh Gunpowder Gin & Tonic

President Eisenhower's Oval Office Treat

Ingredients

  • 1.5oz Bribón Blanco Tequila
  • 0.25oz Chase Elderflower Liqueur
  • 2.5oz orange juice
  • 0.25oz lime juice
  • 0.5oz grenadine

Directions

  1. Combine Tequile, elderflower, and orange juice, and lime in a rocks or highball glass.
  2. Toss with shaker to combine flavors.
  3. In rocks glass top with Grenadine and allow to settle.
  4. Garnish with cherry or orange.
